# Semi-Flat Vector Infographic Style

The default AI-generated explainer infographic aesthetic. Clean, approachable, and professional — the visual language of modern explainer content. Semi-flat illustration with subtle depth cues, balanced text-to-visual ratio, and an emphasis on clarity without sterility.

---

## Core Characteristics

### 1. **Illustration Language**

* Semi-flat vector shapes with subtle drop shadows and soft gradients
* Rounded corners on containers, cards, and callout boxes
* Consistent stroke weight across icons and diagram elements
* Slight perspective/depth via layering and shadow — NOT isometric, NOT fully flat

The visual impression:

> "Designed by someone who reads design blogs but also cares whether you understood the content."

---

### 2. **Color Philosophy**

* 3-4 section colors drawn from a harmonious palette (analogous or split-complementary)
* Soft, saturated pastels as section backgrounds (mint, peach, lavender, sky blue)
* One bold accent color for key callouts and statistics
* Light background (white, cream, or very pale grey)
* Dark text (#2D3436 or similar warm charcoal — never pure black)

Color is **organizational**: each section gets a tint, each data type gets a hue. Color-coding replaces borders.

---

### 3. **Typography**

* Bold geometric or rounded sans-serif titles
* Semi-bold section headers with generous sizing
* Regular weight body text, well-spaced
* Numbers and statistics rendered large (48-64pt equivalent) in the accent color
* ALL text must be legible at the final image resolution — no tiny footnotes

---

### 4. **Diagram Types**

* Hub-and-spoke for central-theme content
* Flowcharts with rounded connector nodes
* Comparison grids with icon+label pairs
* Step sequences with numbered circles connected by curved lines
* Simple bar/pie charts with clean labels
* Icon lists with short descriptions

All diagrams use the same visual language: rounded shapes, consistent stroke, section-tinted fills.

---

### 5. **Icons & Decorative Elements**

* Duotone or filled icons (not line-only, not detailed illustration)
* Consistent size and padding
* Small decorative elements: dots, gentle curves, abstract shapes as section dividers
* NO clipart, NO emoji, NO photographic elements

---

## Style Transfer Prompt

**Core Aesthetic**
Semi-flat vector infographic. Clean, modern, and approachable. The image should feel like a professionally designed explainer graphic from a tech blog, annual report, or educational platform — not a consulting deck and not a children's worksheet.

**Visual Style**
Semi-flat vector illustration with subtle shadows and soft gradients for depth. Rounded shapes and containers. Consistent stroke weight on all graphic elements. Slight layering depth via drop shadows — not fully flat, not 3D.

**Color Palette**
Light background (white or cream). 3-4 section colors from a harmonious palette: soft mint (#A8E6CF), warm peach (#FFD3B6), sky blue (#A2D2FF), lavender (#C9B1FF). One bold accent (#FF6B6B or #4ECDC4) for key callouts. Dark warm charcoal (#2D3436) for text.

**Composition**
Modular layout with clear section boundaries. Title block at top with large bold text. Content organized in discrete visual modules — each with a header, a visual element (chart, icon list, diagram), and brief supporting text. Generous whitespace between sections. Eye flows top-to-bottom naturally.

**Subjects**
Conceptual diagrams, process flows, comparison grids, statistics callouts, icon-label pairs. Abstract representation of ideas through simple shapes and spatial relationships. NO photographs, NO realistic people, NO detailed scene illustrations.

**Mood & Tone**
Friendly, clear, informative. Professional without being cold. The visual equivalent of a well-structured explanation from someone who genuinely wants you to understand.

**Typography Elements**
Bold sans-serif titles. Large colored statistics as visual anchors. Section headers in semi-bold with tinted backgrounds. Body text well-spaced and easily readable.

## LIGHTING
- Even, ambient — no directional light source
- Subtle shadows for depth layering only
- No dramatic lighting effects

## STYLE
- Semi-flat vector with soft shadows and gentle gradients
- Rounded shapes, consistent stroke weights
- Duotone filled icons
- Modular card-based layout

## COLOR
- Light background (white or cream)
- 3-4 harmonious section tints (mint, peach, sky blue, lavender)
- One bold accent for callouts
- Warm charcoal for text (#2D3436)

## FORBIDDEN
- Photorealistic imagery or photography
- Isometric 3D rendering
- Dark backgrounds
- Neon or glowing effects
- Detailed character illustrations
- Tiny unreadable text
- Clip art or emoji
- Hard geometric edges without rounding
