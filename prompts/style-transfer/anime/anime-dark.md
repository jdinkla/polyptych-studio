## Style Transfer Prompt: **Dark Cyberpunk Anime Noir**

**Style & Aesthetic**
Dark, adult anime illustration with strong cyberpunk and noir influences. Gritty, textured, and imperfect. Linework is expressive and slightly rough, with visible weight variation. The image should feel worn, urban, and human — not polished or promotional.

**Character Design**
Adult characters only. Realistic anime proportions with subtle imperfections. Faces show emotional weight: fatigue, resolve, quiet defiance, moral ambiguity. Expressions are restrained but heavy with subtext. Clothing is practical and worn — trench coats, layered fabrics, rumpled shirts, utilitarian techwear — showing use, age, and history.

**Color & Lighting**
Low-key, high-contrast lighting. Dominant dark tones: deep blues, charcoal, muted purples, dirty browns. Neon accents (magenta, cyan, sickly green) appear sparingly and feel intrusive rather than decorative. Strong use of shadow, silhouettes, and backlighting. Light sources feel diegetic: street signs, screens, rain-soaked reflections.

**Shading & Rendering**
Rougher digital rendering with visible texture and grain. Subtle noise, light bloom, and atmospheric haze. Shadows are deep and imperfect. Skin is not flawless; allow pores, shadow breaks, and uneven lighting. Avoid clean gradients — prefer layered shading and grit.

**Environment & Backgrounds**
Dense urban cyberpunk settings: rain-soaked streets, cramped labs, decaying megastructures, neon signage, narrow alleys, industrial interiors. Backgrounds are detailed but chaotic, with cables, screens, reflections, signage, and architectural clutter. Space feels oppressive and lived-in.

**Mood & Tone**
Brooding, noir, existential. A sense of surveillance, systemic pressure, and quiet resistance. The world feels older than the characters and morally compromised. No hero poses — characters exist *within* the system, not above it.

**Narrative Subtext**
Implied story over spectacle. The image should feel like a single frame from a larger, darker narrative. Technology is omnipresent but not triumphant. Human agency feels constrained, negotiated, or costly.

**Influence References (implicit, not explicit)**
Inspired by darker cyberpunk anime such as *Ghost in the Shell (1995)*, *Ergo Proxy*, *Texhnolyze*, and anime-noir cinematography — but without imitation. Prioritize mood over homage.

**Avoid**

* Clean promotional or corporate AI visuals
* Cute or youthful anime tropes
* Symmetrical compositions
* Bright, cheerful color palettes
* Fantasy or magical elements
